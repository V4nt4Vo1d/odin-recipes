Vanilla Cupcake Recipe

Light and fluffy vanilla cupcakes topped with creamy buttercream frosting.
These classic treats are perfect for any occasion, from birthday parties to casual get-togethers.
Made with simple ingredients, they are easy to prepare and sure to delight both kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

Cannabutter

  • 1/2 cup (113 grams) unsalted butter, preferably European-style
  • 1 gram high-quality cannabis flower
  • 1 cup (240 ml) water
  • Fine mesh strainer
  • Small saucepan

Cannabis Vanilla Cupcakes with Buttercream Frosting

  • 1 1/4 cups (160 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Cannabutter (above)
  • 3/4 cup (150 grams) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) whole milk, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (113 grams) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 2 cups (240 grams) powdered sugar, sifted
  • 2-3 tablespoons heavy cream or milk

Instructions:

For the Cannabutter:

  1. Decarboxylate the cannabis: Preheat your oven to 240째F (115째C). Break up the cannabis into small pieces and spread it evenly on a baking sheet. Bake for 30-40 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes, until the cannabis is lightly browned and fragrant.
  2. Infuse the butter: In a small saucepan, combine the decarboxylated cannabis, butter, and water. Simmer on low heat for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally. Do not let it boil.
  3. Strain the mixture: After simmering, strain the butter through a fine mesh strainer into a container, discarding the solid plant material. Allow the cannabutter to cool and solidify.

For the Cupcakes:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the cannabutter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  5. Alternately add the dry ingredients and milk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 18-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow cupcakes to cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

For the Frosting:

  1. In a medium bowl, beat the butter until smooth and creamy. Gradually add the powdered sugar, mixing until well combined.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract and 2-3 tablespoons of heavy cream or milk, and beat until the frosting is light and fluffy.
  3. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, spread or pipe the frosting onto each cupcake. Optionally, garnish with sprinkles or edible glitter.

Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. Enjoy your delicious cannabis-infused vanilla cupcakes!
